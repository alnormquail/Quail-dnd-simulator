PALADIN

Whatever their origin and their mission, paladins are united by their oaths to stand against the forces of evil. A paladin’s oath is a powerful bond — a source of power that turns a devout warrior into a blessed champion.

The Cause of Righteousness  
A paladin swears to uphold justice and righteousness, to stand with the good things of the world against the encroaching darkness, and to hunt the forces of evil wherever they lurk. Although many paladins are devoted to gods of good, a paladin’s power comes as much from a commitment to justice itself as it does from a god.

Beyond the Mundane Life  
Almost by definition, the life of a paladin is an adventuring life. Every paladin lives on the front lines of the cosmic struggle against evil. When they receive the call, these warriors turn from their former occupations and take up arms to fight evil.

Creating a Paladin  
The most important aspect of a paladin character is the nature of his or her holy quest. Although the class features related to your oath don’t appear until you reach 3rd level, plan ahead by reading the oath descriptions at the end of the class. As guardians against the forces of wickedness, paladins are rarely of any evil alignment.

Quick Build: First, Strength should be your highest ability score, followed by Charisma. Second, choose the noble background.

CLASS FEATURES

Hit Points  
Hit Dice: 1d10 per paladin level  
Hit Points at 1st Level: 10 \+ your Constitution modifier  
Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d10 (or 6\) \+ your Constitution modifier per paladin level after 1st

Proficiencies  
Armor: All armor, shields  
Weapons: Simple weapons, martial weapons  
Tools: None  
Saving Throws: Wisdom, Charisma  
Skills: Choose two from Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Persuasion, and Religion

Equipment

1) A martial weapon and a shield or (b) two martial weapons  
2) (a) five javelins or (b) any simple melee weapon  
3) (a) a priest’s pack or (b) an explorer’s pack  
4) Chain mail and a holy symbol

Divine Sense  
As an action, you can open your awareness to detect celestials, fiends, and undead within 60 feet, as well as consecrated or desecrated places/objects. You can use this feature a number of times equal to 1 \+ your Charisma modifier, regaining all uses on a long rest.

Lay on Hands  
You have a pool of healing power equal to your paladin level × 5\. As an action, you can touch a creature to restore hit points from the pool. Alternatively, you can expend 5 hit points to cure one disease or neutralize one poison. No effect on undead or constructs.

Fighting Style (2nd level)  
Choose one: Defense (+1 AC in armor), Dueling (+2 damage with a one-handed melee weapon), Great Weapon Fighting (reroll 1s and 2s on two-handed melee damage), or Protection (impose disadvantage on an attack against an ally within 5 ft; requires a shield).

Spellcasting (2nd level)  
You draw on divine magic to cast spells as a cleric does, preparing a number of spells equal to your Charisma modifier \+ half your paladin level (rounded down, minimum one).  
Spellcasting Ability: Charisma  
Spell save DC \= 8 \+ proficiency bonus \+ Charisma modifier  
Spell attack modifier \= proficiency bonus \+ Charisma modifier  
Spellcasting Focus: a holy symbol

Divine Smite (2nd level)  
When you hit with a melee weapon attack, you can expend a spell slot to deal radiant damage: 2d8 for a 1st-level slot, \+1d8 per slot level above 1st (max 5d8). \+1d8 against undead or fiends (max 6d8).

Divine Health (3rd level)  
The divine magic flowing through you makes you immune to disease.

Sacred Oath (3rd level)  
You swear the Oath of Devotion, the Oath of the Ancients, or the Oath of Vengeance. Your choice grants features at 3rd, 7th, 15th, and 20th level, including oath spells and the Channel Divinity feature.

Ability Score Improvement  
At 4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, increase one ability score by 2 or two scores by 1 (max 20).

Extra Attack (5th level)  
You can attack twice whenever you take the Attack action.

Aura of Protection (6th level)  
You and friendly creatures within 10 feet gain a bonus to saving throws equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum \+1). Range increases to 30 feet at 18th level.

Aura of Courage (10th level)  
You and friendly creatures within 10 feet can’t be frightened while you are conscious. Range increases to 30 feet at 18th level.

Improved Divine Smite (11th level)  
Your melee weapon hits deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage.

Cleansing Touch (14th level)  
As an action, end one spell on yourself or a willing creature you touch. Uses equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1), regained on a long rest.

SACRED OATHS

Breaking Your Oath  
A paladin who breaks a vow typically seeks absolution. A willfully impenitent paladin might be forced to adopt another class or take the Oathbreaker option (Dungeon Master’s Guide).

Oath of Devotion  
Binds a paladin to the loftiest ideals of justice, virtue, and order.  
Tenets: Honesty, Courage, Compassion, Honor, Duty.  
Oath Spells — 3rd: protection from evil and good, sanctuary; 5th: lesser restoration, zone of truth; 9th: beacon of hope, dispel magic; 13th: freedom of movement, guardian of faith; 17th: commune, flame strike.  
Channel Divinity: Sacred Weapon (imbue a weapon with positive energy) and Turn the Unholy (turn fiends and undead).  
Aura of Devotion (7th): You and allies within 10 ft can’t be charmed (30 ft at 18th).  
Purity of Spirit (15th): Always under protection from evil and good.  
Holy Nimbus (20th): Emanate sunlight dealing 10 radiant damage to enemies; advantage on saves vs. fiend/undead spells. Once per long rest.

Oath of the Ancients  
Paladins who love the beautiful and life-giving things of the world.  
Tenets: Kindle the Light, Shelter the Light, Preserve Your Own Light, Be the Light.  
Oath Spells — 3rd: ensnaring strike, speak with animals; 5th: misty step, moonbeam; 9th: plant growth, protection from energy; 13th: ice storm, stoneskin; 17th: commune with nature, tree stride.  
Channel Divinity: Nature’s Wrath (ensnaring vines) and Turn the Faithless (turn fey and fiends).  
Aura of Warding (7th): You and allies within 10 ft have resistance to spell damage (30 ft at 18th).  
Undying Sentinel (15th): Drop to 1 hp instead of 0 (once per long rest); no drawbacks of old age.  
Elder Champion (20th): Transform to regain 10 hp each turn, cast 1-action spells as a bonus action, and impose disadvantage on saves vs. your spells/Channel Divinity. Once per long rest.

Oath of Vengeance  
A solemn commitment to punish those who have committed grievous sins.  
Tenets: Fight the Greater Evil, No Mercy for the Wicked, By Any Means Necessary, Restitution.  
Oath Spells — 3rd: bane, hunter’s mark; 5th: hold person, misty step; 9th: haste, protection from energy; 13th: banishment, dimension door; 17th: hold monster, scrying.  
Channel Divinity: Abjure Enemy (frighten a foe) and Vow of Enmity (advantage on attacks against a chosen creature).  
Relentless Avenger (7th): Move up to half your speed after an opportunity attack hit, without provoking.  
Soul of Vengeance (15th): Reaction melee attack against a creature under your Vow of Enmity when it attacks.  
Avenging Angel (20th): Transform for 1 hour to gain a 60-ft flying speed and an aura of menace that frightens enemies. Once per long rest.
